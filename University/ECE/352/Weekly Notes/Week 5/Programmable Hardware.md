#### Types of devices:
1. FPGAs (Field-Programmable Gate Arrays) **(Mostly Used one)**
2. ROMs, PLDs (PAL/PLA)

#### Tradeoffs of using Programmable hardware:
- More area
- Adds delay
- Consumes more power/energy
